About Me

My name is Ammon Nelson, if you hadn't already guessed. I was born in Ogden, Utah, but I didn't stay there long. In fact, I lived out most of my childhood and teen life in Allen, Texas. I have quite a big family being one of 8 children! There is never a dull moment in my family! Always something going on in the Nelson home! And the family seems to be growing at an alarming rate! Lots of new little baby additions!

I really love sports! As a kid I played soccer (it wasnt too bad), baseball (which I hated), basketball (was totally awesome), and football (my favorite). Growing up in Texas, football stuck with me and I played that all through highschool. I played on the Allen High School football team as a wide reciever and that was an amazing experience and is something that I will never forget.

After highschool I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ Of Latter-Day Saints in Redlands, California. This is the part of my life where things get awesome because I met my beatufiul wife there in California. It took me two years to find out that I was going to marry her! And, boy oh boy am I lucky that I found her! I am now royalty because I got married to the Queen of the Galaxy. And yes, she is the most beautiful woman to ever exist! We have the documents to prove it. I became the luckiest man ever on January 2nd, 2021.

I am currently attending school at BYU-Idaho and am studying Computer Science. I really love working with computers. Making them do cool things like this website has always been really intresting to me! I've always had a love for computers and what we are able to do on them. I love electronic music and I always thought it was amazing that a computer could make music and do many of the other things we ask them to do each day!

Currently my hobbies are working on my computer programming knowledge, listening to music, and hanging out with my wife and friends. My favorite music artist is EDEN. He is very talented at making musical masterpieces. One of my favorite songs by him would have to be Good Morning. I also really love video games and the work that goes into those! Everyone loves a good story or a good first person shooter! I am a fan of older games as well! The nostalgia is awesome! I also want to have a side hobby of photgraphy because I think having memories that you can look back on is super cool!

One day I hope to be really skilled in using my knowledge of computer programming to build the future in some awesome ways! The world is moving towards technology at a unprecedented speed and I hope to be able to look out the front window in the driver seat enjoying the ride! There is so much to look forward to in the future of technology! There are so many ideas and things to work on!

Here's to the future!
